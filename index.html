    • Process

    Learn: Violence is a huge problem across America, but the causes and solutions are a mystery. On the learn page, you can learn about violence so you have the information you need find solutions.

    Discuss: Friends and neighbors discuss problems like violence, but most people don not realize the best solutions come from conversations with some different than you. The bigger the difference, the better. On the forum, you can find people with different beliefs and backgrounds and discuss how to create a more peaceful community.

    Solve: We know you have a lot of ideas, so pick your best solution to violence in your community and turn your idea into an action plan.

    Impact: Get started with your action plan or give your plan to legislators, public safety councils, or neighborhood councils. We guide you and help bring your action plan to life.
